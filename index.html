About Me

I'm a platform engineer at IBM with a lifelong interest in visual effects and the creativity behind filmmaking. My work sits at the intersection of technical infrastructure and creative tools — full-stack development, ML applications, and pipeline-adjacent engineering. I lead a disability advocacy group at IBM and I'm finishing a degree apprenticeship in Software Engineering at the University of Exeter. I've wanted to understand how films are made since first realising you could use technology to bring imagery previously only found in books to life. It feels like the perfect crossover of my technical skills and creative interests. Right now, I am looking at how my current education and experience could inform a future career path in film - specifically technical aspects like pipeline development.

When I'm not coding, you can find me creating digital art, knitting while watching the clone wars, dangling from rocks, or rollerblading through London.
